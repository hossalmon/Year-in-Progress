About Year in Progress

Year in Progress (est. 2025) is a visual motivation tool designed to help you reflect, plan, and stay inspired throughout the year. By showing how much of the year has passed, it serves as a gentle reminder to make the most of your time.

  • Track progress across year, month, week, or day.
  • Toggle tools like a quote of the day, timer, and more.
  • A personal timeline feature and real-life products coming soon to mark milestones, goals, and reflections.
